Overview

Covered laundry bay at the back of the house. Washing machine provision with drain point and wall tap. A back door opens directly to the rear drying yard in the 8ft setback — private and not road-visible. A small utility sink for hand-washing and soaking. Ventilated by a Jali window above the machine.

Interior Design Note The utility sink is a single-piece Kota stone basin, wall-mounted at 85cm height. Drain board integrated. The back door is a solid-core teak half-door (top half can be kept open for ventilation while bottom is closed). Clothesline hooks installed on the rear compound wall in the drying yard. The WM sits on a raised 10cm plinth to allow front-load door to open fully and reduce bending.
