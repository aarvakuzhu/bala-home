Overview

Open canopy structure on the west side of the compound — not enclosed with solid walls. A flat RCC or sloped metal-sheet canopy is supported on four RCC columns. The east face is completely open to the driveway. The south face has a roller shutter at the compound wall boundary. Two cars park side by side. Two dedicated bike bays in the north portion. A pedestrian door connects directly to the foyer.
