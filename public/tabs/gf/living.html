Overview

The Sunken Living Room drops 18 inches below the main GF level via 3 wide steps. The lower level creates a natural enclosure — an intimate conversation zone without walls. A low Kadappa stone parapet caps the step edge. TV wall faces north. The sofa group faces the Thinnai — guests in the sunken zone look out through the foyer toward the entry.

Interior Design Note The step parapet doubles as a ledge — Kadappa stone cap at 45cm height, wide enough to sit on. The TV niche is recessed 20cm into the north wall — teak frame, no protruding unit. Underfloor LED strip at step base for night lighting. The L-sofa wraps the south-west corner. The conversation pit effect is complete — the room feels enclosed by level change, not walls.
