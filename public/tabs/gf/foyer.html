Overview

The foyer is a privacy screen — Kitchen and Dining are completely invisible from the entry door. The Sunken Living Room draws the eye naturally east. A small niche for keys and shoes sits beside the Pooja room door. The double-leaf teak main door is flanked by narrow jali windows.
