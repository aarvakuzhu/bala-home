Overview

East-facing per Vastu — the first morning light falls on the altar. The Pooja room also acts as a visual screen, hiding the Kitchen and Dining from the entry door. Marble floor, jali stone screen on the east window, built-in teak shelf altar. A small copper drain in the floor for ritual water.

Interior Design Note The altar occupies the full north wall — 3 teak shelves, stepped like a temple sanctum. Bottom shelf for heavy brass idols, middle for daily puja items, top for framed photographs. The east jali stone screen is 60×90cm — hand-carved floral pattern, allows morning light to filter through in patterns across the marble floor. Ochre lime wash on walls gives a warm sanctum quality.
