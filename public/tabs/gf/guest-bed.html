Overview

Ground floor guest bedroom near the foyer — accessible without disturbing the main household. A double/queen bed with headboard against the north wall. Built-in wardrobe on the west wall. The room has a Jali window on the east wall for cross-ventilation. Private attached bathroom accessible from inside the room.
