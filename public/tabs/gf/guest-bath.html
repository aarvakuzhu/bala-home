Overview

Private bathroom attached to the guest bedroom. Wet and dry zone separated by a glass panel. Wall-hung WC and floating vanity keep the floor visible and easy to clean. Rainfall shower head in the wet zone. Jali ventilation on the outer wall. Stone-look large format tile throughout.
