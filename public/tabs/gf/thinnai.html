Overview

The Thinnai is the traditional Tamil raised front sit-out — a liminal space between the street and the home. Visitors rest here without entering. The Kolam pattern on the threshold floor is drawn fresh each morning. Elevated 6 inches above the path, with a low stone parapet bench on three sides.

The canopy soffit is lime-plastered white with exposed teak rafters. The parapet bench runs three sides — Kadappa stone cap, traditional corbelled profile. No furniture — the bench is the furniture.
