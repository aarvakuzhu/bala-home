Overview

L-shaped kitchen with the cook facing the dining room. A counter-height half-wall separates the two spaces — solid masonry below counter level, open above. The fridge sits near the half-wall partition, accessible from the dining side for drinks. Hob on the north counter, sink east. A chimney hood above the hob vents to the exterior.

Interior Design Note The feature wall (behind the hob) is a deep forest green — Benjamin Moore "Hunter Green" equivalent. Subway tile backsplash wraps the counter on all sides in a classic 75×150mm cream format. Brass taps and cabinet handles tie to the rest of the house metalwork. The overhead cabinets extend to 2.6m height — a sliding ladder provides access to top shelves. Under-cabinet LED strip lighting.
