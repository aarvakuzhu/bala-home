Overview

The first floor continuation of the GF Nadumuttam courtyard. At FF level, the courtyard becomes an open void with a wrought iron railing — you look down to the Tulsi planter and Cuddapah stone floor below. The full sky is visible above. Light and rain travel the full height of the house through this shaft. The railing is the same kolam-pattern iron as the stair and balcony.

Interior Design Note The railing at FF level is 1.05m tall — child-safe with vertical infill bars at 10cm spacing in addition to the kolam decorative panel. The brass top rail cap runs the full perimeter. Looking down from here to the Tulsi planter below is one of the defining spatial experiences of the house — the full 11ft height visible. At night, the diya lamp on the GF Nadumuttam floor is visible from FF as a point of warm light.
