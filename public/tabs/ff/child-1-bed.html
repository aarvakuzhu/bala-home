Overview

The first child bedroom faces east — morning light, good for waking up. Single bed (convertible to bunk when needed). A full-width study desk with overhead shelving is built into the north wall. A 2-door wardrobe with a full-length mirror. The room is designed to grow — adaptable from nursery to study room to teenager's retreat.

Interior Design Note The desk is 2.4m wide — full-span north wall — with 40cm deep overhead shelving at 1.6m height (reachable while seated). Under-desk cable management built in. The terracotta ceiling with a warm white cove creates a cave-like comfort — safe and contained. A bunk bed conversion kit is pre-installed in the wall frame — no drilling needed.
