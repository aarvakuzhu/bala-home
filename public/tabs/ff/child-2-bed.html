Overview

The second child bedroom mirrors Room 1 in size and planning logic but has its own distinct character — facing south for afternoon light and views to the compound trees. Same desk and wardrobe layout but in a different palette. Designed to feel like a different world from Room 1 while being equally functional.

Interior Design Note Deep forest green behind the headboard — a dramatic departure from Room 1's terracotta ceiling. This room feels like a forest hideout. The mustard wardrobe panels are hand-painted — a local craftsman's finish, not laminate. The Athangudi floor pattern is a pinwheel in mustard and ivory.
