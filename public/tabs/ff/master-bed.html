Overview

The master bedroom occupies the full west zone of the first floor. A king bed with a full-height upholstered headboard wall faces the south windows. The full-width wardrobe wall runs from end to end — solid cabinetry with a full-length mirror section in the centre. A narrow dressing corridor at the south end leads east into the ensuite. 10 ft ceiling with concealed cove lighting.
