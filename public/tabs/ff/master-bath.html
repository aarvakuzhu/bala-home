Overview

The master ensuite is accessed via the dressing corridor — a deliberate sequence of wardrobe → dressing → bathing. A freestanding Kadappa stone bathtub faces the east window. A separate walk-in shower with a niche. Double floating vanity with under-lit Corian top. Wall-hung WC in a private nook. All brass fixtures.

Interior Design Note The Kadappa stone bathtub is custom-carved — 1.7m long, 75cm wide, 60cm deep. The stone is naturally cool and holds temperature well. The shower niche has three integral shelves cut into the wall. The double vanity is 120cm wide — two under-counter basins, one per person. The LED mirror cabinet is 120cm wide with integrated demister, shaver socket, and storage. Radiant floor heating under the marble (Chennai nights can be cool in December).
